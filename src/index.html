Common Questions

Will rain effect your outside services?

Rain only effects outside services if it's raining while we are applying pesticide. If it's raining we will not carry out a treatment. If rain has stopped then we can continue the treatment.

Do I have to leave my home when you provide service?

In most cases you won't have to leave your home, but you may have to leave the area where your pest technician is working.
